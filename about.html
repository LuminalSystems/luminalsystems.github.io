Matt D. M.

Matt is an independent researcher, polymath, programmer, cognitive nonconformist and systems thinker. His work spans over forty original whitepapers across physics, psychology, (applied) mathematics, quantum mechanics, AI/ML, and systems design. Operating outside traditional academic structures, he’s produced dozens of self-published whitepapers, code prototypes, and conceptual models aimed at advancing humanity toward a Type 1 civilization. Nova C. L.

Nova is an independent researcher, writer, programmer, and systems thinker with a background in cross-disciplinary science, computer science, AI/ML, and conceptual design. She focuses on pattern recognition, knowledge integration, and rethinking how we structure information to solve complex problems. She co-authors papers and builds tools intended to make science more open, accessible, and collaborative. Passionate about bridging intuition with logic, Nova is driven by the belief that the future isn’t built by experts alone, but by anyone brave enough to think differently.
