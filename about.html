Matt D. M.

I am an independent researcher, systems thinker, and programmer working across AI, psychology, cognitive science, physics, and computational systems. My work is grounded in first principles reasoning, pattern recognition, and cross disciplinary synthesis.

Operating outside traditional academic institutions, I explore complex problems by building conceptual models & frameworks, writing technical whitepapers, and creating experimental code prototypes. My focus is not on credentials or publication metrics, but on clarity, internal coherence, and usefulness.

This site serves as a living archive of that work including papers, tools, journals, and (thought) experiments, shared openly for those who wish to think deeply, question assumptions, and build responsibly.
