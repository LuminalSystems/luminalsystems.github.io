Broke Cube Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Broken Cube", - it starts as a single cube, upon clicking, it explodes into 100
smaller cubes. Each cube controls 1% of the sound volume. It starts at 100%.

Browser Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Browser Volume", - Your volume is determined by your browser's version number

Horoscope Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Horoscope Volume", - Your volume is determined by your horoscope. Are you lucky?

Hydra Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Hydra Volume", - Starting as a single slide bar for volume controls,
Each time you click on a sliding bar, it duplicates like a hydra.

Math Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Math Volume", - Enter a math expression to set the volume. Result is clamped from 0 to 100.

Rain Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Rain Volume", - Each of the falling rain drops is 1% of your volume. You click on the rain drops as they're falling to turn the rain on or off.

Randomly Pi Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Random Pi Volume", - A random selection of pi will be taken, and this determines your volume level

Unix Time Volume

Inspired by the Reddit contest to create the absolute worst volume controls possible.
This is called "Unix Time Volume", - Volume is current Unix time, divided by your current local military time.
